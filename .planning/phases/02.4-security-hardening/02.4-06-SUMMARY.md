## Accomplishments

Verified the Phase 02.4 mitigations that the `apps/api` Vitest harness cannot cover (DB-level constraints, client-side role gating, and live R2 upload behavior):

- **UAT 1 — DB data-integrity constraints (D-09/D-10/D-13):** PASS. Slug reuse-after-soft-delete, one-primary-image-per-listing, and FK `onDelete` behavior confirmed against live Postgres.
- **UAT 2 — Client role gating (D-04/D-05):** PASS. RoleGuard denies retailers and scopes manufacturer/admin per the route→role map in the browser.
- **UAT 3 — End-to-end upload validation (D-06/D-07):** PASS (on re-test). Initially FAILED on two gaps, both closed and re-verified:
  - Gap 1 — R2 does not support presigned POST → reverted to presigned PUT with exact signed `ContentLength` (plan 02.4-07).
  - Gap 2 — server rejections were swallowed → surfaced in the uploader UI (plan 02.4-08).
  - Re-test checks: valid JPEG/PNG < 5 MB uploads end-to-end (no CORS); SVG rejected with visible error; > 5 MB rejected with visible error.
