## Chosen FK onDelete Policy (D-13, explicit for plan-checker)

| FK | onDelete | Verified in DB (confdeltype) |
|----|----------|------------------------------|
| `brands.companyId → companies.id` | `restrict` | `r` |
| `inventorySkus.listingId → brandListings.id` | `cascade` | `c` |
| `brandListingImages.listingId → brandListings.id` | `cascade` | `c` |
| `listingCategories.listingId → brandListings.id` | `cascade` | `c` |
| `listingCategories.categoryId → categories.id` | `restrict` | `r` |

Rationale: `cascade` for rows that are owned parts of a listing (SKUs, images, join rows); `restrict` for cross-entity references so a company or category hard-delete fails loudly rather than silently orphaning/stripping catalog data. `set null` was rejected — all five columns are `NOT NULL`.
