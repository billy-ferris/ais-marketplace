<objective>
Add the locked validation-timing behavior and required-field indicators to the three react-hook-form forms (ListingForm, BrandDialog, CategoryDialog) by extending the existing raw register/errors pattern with three tiny reusable helpers — NOT by adopting shadcn's Radix-based Form components.

Purpose: Deliver "validate on blur, clear on valid change, catch-all on submit with focus" plus red asterisks and textarea character counters across all RHF forms with a consistent, reviewable pattern.
Output: A FormFieldHelpers.tsx module and three updated forms.

Note on the `status` select: CONTEXT.md's Required Field Indicators decision literally lists "(brand, status, categories)" as required selects. Two of those are intentionally NOT given the required-asterisk treatment — this is a deliberate, resolved decision (see 02.3-VALIDATION.md), not a silent scope cut: (1) listing `status` has a schema default `'draft'`, is pre-populated on the create form, can never be emptied, and therefore can never fire a required error — it gets NO asterisk; (2) listing `categoryIds` is optional in the schema and managed outside RHF — no asterisk (critical scope decision #2). The genuinely-required selects that DO get the asterisk + on-blur treatment are the listing `brandId` and the brand-dialog `companyId` (manufacturer).
</objective>
