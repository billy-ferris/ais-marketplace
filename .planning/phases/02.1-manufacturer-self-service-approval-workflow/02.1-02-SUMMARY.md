## Decisions Made
- Resend email failures are caught and logged but never throw -- email is fire-and-forget to avoid blocking approval actions (per RESEARCH.md Pitfall 4)
- Manufacturer listing GET / switched from leftJoin to innerJoin on brands table to enable reliable company scoping via brands.companyId filter
- Submit endpoint accepts draft, rejected, and archived as valid source statuses (archived -> pending_approval enables manufacturer reactivation with re-approval per user decision)
- All approval state transitions use optimistic locking (WHERE status = expected_status) to prevent race conditions (per RESEARCH.md Pitfall 3)
